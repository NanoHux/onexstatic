Staking ONEX

When users stake their tokens with ONEX, they essentially lock them in our wallet as collateral to demonstrate their commitment to the network. By doing so, they become eligible to be chosen as a validator to validate and create new blocks. The chance of being selected as a validator is typically proportional to the number of tokens they have staked. Staking helps maintain the network's security and efficiency, as validators have a financial incentive to act honestly and follow the protocol rules.

In return for staking their tokens and participating in block validation, users can earn staking rewards.

Overall, token staking offers a way for cryptocurrency holders to earn passive income by contributing to the security and consensus of the ONEX proof-of-stake blockchain network.
